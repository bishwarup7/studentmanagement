About the Computer Department

The Computer Science & Technology Department at Faridpur Polytechnic Institute is dedicated to delivering high-quality technical education in the field of computer science and engineering. Established with the vision to produce skilled computer professionals, the department offers a well-structured Diploma in Engineering program under the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB).

Our curriculum is designed to meet the growing demands of the IT industry. We focus on both theoretical and practical knowledge including programming, database management, networking, software engineering, and web development. The department is equipped with modern computer labs, experienced faculty members, and up-to-date resources.
